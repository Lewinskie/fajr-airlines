Humanitarian and Relief Services

Dragonfly offers humanitarian services to save the lives of millions of people caught up in humanitarian disasters. We ensure quick and safe access to affected areas. We also provide air services to transport humanitarian personnel and supplies, as well as to perform medical and security evacuations as required.

Learn More

Our humanitarian services are provided in partnership with leading aid agencies. We have the experience and resources to respond quickly to any crisis, and to deliver aid to those in need. Contact us to find out more about our humanitarian and relief services.
